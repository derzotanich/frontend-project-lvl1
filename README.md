### Game: "Even check"
The user gets a random number. He needs to answer "yes" if the number is even, or "no" if it is not.

Example:

brain-even

Welcome to the Brain Games!
May I have your name? Sam
Hello, Sam!
Answer "yes" if the number is even, otherwise answer "no".
Question: 15
Your answer: no
Correct!
Question: 6
Your answer: yes
Correct!
Question: 7
Your answer: no
Correct!
Congratulations, Sam!
